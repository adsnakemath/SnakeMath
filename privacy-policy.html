Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 28, 2026 · Version 1.0
TL;DR — The Short Version
  • We only collect what's needed to run the game
  • We never sell your data to anyone
  • Your Solana wallet address is used only for token withdrawals
  • You can delete your account and data at any time
01 —

Who We Are

SnakeMath is a web-based play-to-earn game published on the Solana dApp Store. We operate this service and are responsible for the data described in this policy. Contact: ad.snakemath@gmail.com

02 —

Data We Collect

We collect the minimum data necessary to operate the game and distribute rewards:

Data Purpose Required?
Username Account identity and leaderboard display Yes
Password (hashed) Account authentication Yes
Solana wallet address SKR token withdrawal processing For withdrawals
Game scores & history Leaderboard ranking and reward calculation Yes
Transaction records Deposit/withdrawal history and dispute resolution Yes
Achievement data In-game progression tracking Yes

We do not collect email addresses, phone numbers, real names, or any government-issued identification.

03 —

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to:

  • Authenticate your account and maintain sessions
  • Display scores and rankings on the leaderboard
  • Calculate and distribute weekly SKR pool rewards
  • Process withdrawal requests to your Solana wallet
  • Track game achievements and XP progression
  • Detect and prevent cheating or fraudulent activity
04 —

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We do not share your data with advertisers or third-party marketing platforms.

Limited sharing occurs only in these circumstances:

  • Blockchain transactions — Solana wallet addresses and transaction hashes are publicly visible on the Solana blockchain by nature of the protocol
  • Legal requirements — if required by applicable law or valid legal process
05 —

Data Security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect your data:

  • Passwords are stored using cryptographic hashing (never in plaintext)
  • Sensitive API endpoints require authenticated sessions
  • Withdrawal requests are validated server-side before processing

No system is 100% secure. By using SnakeMath, you acknowledge that you provide your data at your own risk.

06 —

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about your account
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Update your Solana wallet address at any time via Settings

To exercise these rights, email us at ad.snakemath@gmail.com.

07 —

Children's Privacy

SnakeMath is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a minor has registered an account, please contact us immediately.

08 —

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Effective Date" at the top will reflect the latest revision. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
