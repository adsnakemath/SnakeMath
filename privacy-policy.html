Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 27, 2026

SnakeMath is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights as a user.

1. Who We Are

SnakeMath is an independently developed application, not operated by a registered company. The developer can be contacted at: ad.snakemath@gmail.com

2. Data We Collect

We collect only the minimum data necessary to operate the App:

Data TypePurposeRequired?
Email addressAccount creation, password reset, notificationsYes
Username / display nameLeaderboard display, profileYes
Solana wallet addressDeposit/withdrawal verificationOnly for token features
Game scores & XPLeaderboard, achievements, reward poolYes
Transaction hashesBlockchain deposit verification via Helius APIOnly for deposits
Device/browser infoApp functionality, session managementAutomatic

We do not collect your private keys, seed phrases, or any sensitive wallet credentials.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To create and manage your user account
  • To display your scores on the leaderboard and track achievements
  • To process SKR token deposits and withdrawals
  • To send transactional emails (password reset, withdrawal confirmation, notifications)
  • To detect cheating or fraud and maintain fair gameplay
  • To improve the App's performance and fix bugs

4. Third-Party Services

SnakeMath integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Helius API — Used to verify Solana blockchain transactions. Helius may log transaction data per their own privacy policy.
  • Gmail / SMTP — Used to send account-related emails. Google may process email metadata.
  • Solana Blockchain — All on-chain transactions (deposits, withdrawals) are publicly visible on the Solana blockchain by nature.

We do not sell or share your personal data with advertisers or unrelated third parties.

5. Blockchain Data

⚠️ Transactions on the Solana blockchain are public and permanent. Your wallet address and transaction history associated with SnakeMath are visible on the public blockchain. This is inherent to blockchain technology and outside our control.

6. Data Retention

  • Account data is retained for as long as your account is active
  • Game history and scores are retained indefinitely for leaderboard integrity
  • If you request account deletion, we will remove your personal data (email, username) within 30 days, except where retention is required for legal or fraud-prevention purposes

7. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures including:

  • Passwords are hashed before storage (never stored in plain text)
  • Access to config files and sensitive directories is restricted via server rules
  • Deposit transactions are verified via signed API calls to prevent replay attacks
  • API endpoints use authentication tokens and prepared SQL statements to prevent injection

8. Children's Privacy

SnakeMath is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If we become aware that a minor has created an account, we will delete their data promptly.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction — Request correction of inaccurate personal data
  • Deletion — Request deletion of your account and personal data
  • Opt-out — Unsubscribe from non-essential email notifications

To exercise any of these rights, email us at ad.snakemath@gmail.com

10. Cookies and Local Storage

The App uses browser local storage and session tokens to maintain your login state. No advertising cookies or third-party tracking cookies are used.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance.
