// 01 — About the Game

What is SnakeMath?

SnakeMath blends the timeless snake arcade with mental arithmetic. A math question appears each round — steer your snake to collect the correct answer tile before time runs out. Wrong tiles shrink your snake. Right answers grow it and earn you XP in-game.


At the end of each month, top XP scorers receive SKR token rewards from the community Reward Share Pool — settled on the Solana blockchain. Available only through the official Solana dApp Store.

// 04 — How to Play

Getting Started

  1. 01
    Open SnakeMath on the Solana dApp Store and connect your Solana wallet.
  2. 02
    A math question appears at the top — three answer tiles appear on the grid.
  3. 03
    Steer your snake to the tile with the correct answer before time runs out.
  4. 04
    Correct answer → snake grows + streak increases → earn XP. Wrong tile → snake shrinks.
  5. 05
    Wall collision or time-out ends the round. Best score saves on-chain automatically.
  6. 06
    Each month, top XP scorers automatically receive SKR token rewards from the Reward Share Pool.
